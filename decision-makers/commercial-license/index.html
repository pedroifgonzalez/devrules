    A: Yes! You can use DevRules for non-production purposes indefinitely to evaluate it. Many companies run 30-90 day pilots.

    A: You'll need to purchase a commercial license at that time. We offer a 30-day grace period after crossing the threshold.

    Q: Do you offer discounts?¶

    A: Yes, we offer: - Startup discounts (< 50 employees) - Non-profit discounts (50% off) - Educational institution discounts (75% off) - Multi-year discounts (up to 20% off)

    A: Yes, we accept purchase orders from established companies. Payment terms are typically Net 30.

    Q: What payment methods do you accept?¶

    A: We accept: - Wire transfer / ACH - Credit card - Purchase orders - PayPal (for smaller amounts)

    Q: Is the source code still available?¶

    A: Yes! Even commercial license holders have full access to the source code. BSL is "source available."

    Q: Can we modify the code?¶

    A: Yes! You can modify DevRules for your internal use. Commercial licenses include modification rights.

    Q: Can we redistribute our modified version?¶

    A: Not externally. You can deploy internally within your organization. Redistribution requires a separate OEM license.

    Q: What if we want custom features?¶

    A: Custom features can be added to Enterprise License agreements. We'll provide a quote based on scope.

    Q: Is support included?¶

    A: Email support is included in all commercial licenses. Enterprise licenses include SLA-backed support and priority response times.

    Q: Can we get a refund?¶
